It was mixed with <a href="/wiki/Tibanna" title="Tibanna">tibanna gas</a>, compressed, and <a href="/wiki/Carbon_freezing" title="Carbon freezing" class="mw-redirect">flash-frozen</a> into blocks for transportation. </p> <nav id="toc" class="toc"><div id="toctitle"><h2>Contents</h2><span class="toctoggle">[<a href="#" class="internal" id="togglelink" data-show="show" data-hide="hide">show</a>]</span></div><ol></ol></nav> <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="editsection"><a href="/wiki/Carbonite?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit History section"><img src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" class="sprite edit-pencil" />Edit</a></span></h2> <p>The <a href="/wiki/Empress_Teta_system" title="Empress Teta system">Empress Teta system</a> was a source of carbonite. The discovery that carbonite could be used to freeze <a href="/wiki/Spacer" title="Spacer">spacers</a> for their long journeys among the stars put an end to the <a href="/wiki/Generation_ship" title="Generation ship">generation ships</a>. <a href="/wiki/Empress_Teta" title="Empress Teta">Koros Major</a> became a boomworld and one of the strongholds of the <a href="/wiki/Mining_Guild" title="Mining Guild">Mining Guild</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-0">[1]</a></sup> </p> <figure class="article-thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 150px">  <a href="http://img1.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20110318002347/starwars/images/3/34/Hansicle-TSWA.jpg"  class="image image-thumbnail"      ><img src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D"   alt="Hansicle-TSWA"   class="thumbimage lzy lzyPlcHld "    data-image-key="Hansicle-TSWA.jpg"  data-image-name="Hansicle-TSWA.jpg"   data-src="http://img2.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20110318002347/starwars/images/thumb/3/34/Hansicle-TSWA.jpg/150px-Hansicle-TSWA.jpg"    width="150"    height="320"        onload="if(typeof ImgLzy===&#39;object&#39;){ImgLzy.load(this)}"   ><noscript><img src="http://img2.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20110318002347/starwars/images/thumb/3/34/Hansicle-TSWA.jpg/150px-Hansicle-TSWA.jpg"   alt="Hansicle-TSWA"   class="thumbimage "    data-image-key="Hansicle-TSWA.jpg"  data-image-name="Hansicle-TSWA.jpg"     width="150"    height="320"         ></noscript></a>   <figcaption>       <a href="/wiki/File:Hansicle-TSWA.jpg" class="sprite info-icon"></a>             <p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Han_Solo" title="Han Solo">Han Solo</a>, frozen in a carbonite block</p>     </figcaption> </figure> <p>The <a href="/wiki/Planet" title="Planet">planet</a> of <a href="/wiki/Polus" title="Polus">Polus</a> also had vast quantities of the metal alloy in its frozen wastelands. The <a href="/wiki/Pyn%27gani" title="Pyn&#39;gani">inhabitants of Polus</a> played a major role in the freezing process and mining.<sup id="cite_ref-EAW_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-EAW-1">[2]</a></sup> </p><p>There were numerous times when individuals encased themselves in carbonite. In <a href="/wiki/33_BBY" title="33 BBY">33 BBY</a>, a year before the <a href="/wiki/Trade_Federation" title="Trade Federation">Trade Federation</a> invaded <a href="/wiki/Naboo" title="Naboo">Naboo</a>, <a href="/wiki/I-5YQ" title="I-5YQ">I-5YQ</a> froze himself and <a href="/wiki/Lorn_Pavan" title="Lorn Pavan">Lorn Pavan</a> in carbonite to survive an explosion set off by <a href="/wiki/Darsha_Assant" title="Darsha Assant">Darsha Assant</a> during her duel with <a href="/wiki/Darth_Maul" title="Darth Maul">Darth Maul</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Shadow_Hunter_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Shadow_Hunter-2">[3]</a></sup> Sometime during the <a href="/wiki/Clone_Wars" title="Clone Wars">Clone Wars</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jedi_Knight" title="Jedi Knight">Jedi Knight</a> <a href="/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker" title="Anakin Skywalker">Anakin Skywalker</a> encased himself and his team in carbonite to be <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Gwori" title="Battle of Gwori">sent</a> undetected to the planet <a href="/wiki/Gwori" title="Gwori">Gwori</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-sod_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sod-3">[4]</a></sup> and later used this method on another <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Lola_Sayu" title="Battle of Lola Sayu">mission</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-citadel_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-citadel-4">[5]</a></sup> </p><p><a href="/wiki/Filba" title="Filba">Filba</a> used carbonite for smuggling <a href="/wiki/Bota" title="Bota">bota</a> harvested on <a href="/wiki/Drongar" title="Drongar">Drongar</a> during the Clone Wars to preserve its quality. Some of <a href="/wiki/Coruscant" title="Coruscant">Coruscant</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Skyhook" title="Skyhook">skyhooks</a> were tethered to the surface with carbonite nanofiber.<sup id="cite_ref-coruscantii_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-coruscantii-5">[6]</a></sup> </p><p>In some locales, carbonite was also used to preserve the bodies of the dead. On Coruscant, <a href="/wiki/Senator" title="Senator">Senator</a> <a href="/wiki/Viento" title="Viento">Viento</a> was buried in this manner in the <a href="/wiki/Senatorial_Tombs" title="Senatorial Tombs">Senatorial Tombs</a>. <a href="/wiki/Han_Solo" title="Han Solo">Han Solo</a> was frozen in carbonite on Darth Vader's orders to test whether a <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">Human</a> could survive Bespin's freezing process before his plan to freeze <a href="/wiki/Luke_Skywalker" title="Luke Skywalker">Luke Skywalker</a>. The modifications to the freezing chamber were successful in that Solo lived through the freezing process and entered into <a href="/wiki/Suspended_animation" title="Suspended animation">suspended animation</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-EPV_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-EPV-6">[7]</a></sup> This state also served as an art piece of Vader's creation. <a href="/wiki/Boba_Fett" title="Boba Fett">Boba Fett</a> dubbed it, <i>"<a href="/wiki/Man_in_Carbonite" title="Man in Carbonite">Man in Carbonite</a>"</i>, enticing Jabba to pay a significantly higher price for Solo. <a href="/wiki/Savage_Opress" title="Savage Opress">Savage Opress</a> also became a subject of art in this manner prior to his death. <a href="/wiki/Moruth_Doole" title="Moruth Doole">Moruth Doole</a> froze the body of the former warden of the <a href="/wiki/Kessel" title="Kessel">Kessel</a> prison system in carbonite in his office as a trophy.<sup id="cite_ref-JS_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JS-7">[8]</a></sup> While imprisoned in carbonite, a <a href="/wiki/Force-sensitive" title="Force-sensitive">Force user</a> could not use <a href="/wiki/The_Force" title="The Force">the Force</a>, or interact with it on any significant level, making it a useful <a href="/wiki/Jedi" title="Jedi">Jedi</a> containment tool. A common part of a <a href="/wiki/Hyperdrive" title="Hyperdrive">hyperdrive</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Carbonite_insert" title="Carbonite insert">carbonite insert</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DEII_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DEII-8">[9]</a></sup> </p><p>The usage of carbonite was obvious even for military purposes and <a href="/wiki/Carbonite_gun" title="Carbonite gun">carbonite guns</a> were made, used to spray a target in carbonite to freeze and paralyse the enemy. Those existed since during the time of the Old Republic,<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[10]</a></sup> while early in the <a href="/wiki/Galactic_Civil_War" title="Galactic Civil War">Galactic Civil War</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Galactic_Empire" title="Galactic Empire">Galactic Empire</a> used <a href="/wiki/Carbonite_war_droid" title="Carbonite war droid">Carbonite war droids</a>. There was at least one case of Carbonite war droids spraying an entire village in carbonite. During the <a href="/wiki/Time" title="Time">time</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Galactic_Civil_War" title="Second Galactic Civil War">Second Galactic Civil War</a>, <a href="/wiki/Boba_Fett" title="Boba Fett">Boba Fett</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mirta_Gev" title="Mirta Gev">Mirta Gev</a> discovered that Fett's <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">wife</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sintas_Vel" title="Sintas Vel">Sintas Vel</a>, had not died, but had been imprisoned in carbonite.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">[11]</a></sup> The usage of such carbonite-based weapons continued through the Civil War.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">[12]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">[13]</a></sup> </p><p>In <a href="/wiki/43_ABY" title="43 ABY">43.5 ABY</a>, just a couple of <a href="/wiki/Year" title="Year">years</a> after the end of the Second Galactic Civil War, <a href="/wiki/Chief_of_State_(Galactic_Alliance)" title="Chief of State (Galactic Alliance)">Chief of State</a> <a href="/wiki/Natasi_Daala" title="Natasi Daala">Natasi Daala</a> utilized carbonite to imprison violent Jedi that were succumbing to a mysterious <a href="/wiki/Force_psychosis" title="Force psychosis">Force psychosis</a>. Carbonite was seen as the only means to contain the Jedi due to the relatively safe incapacitating effect of carbonite encasement. There were some <a href="/wiki/Force-sensitive" title="Force-sensitive">Force-sensitive</a> beings that could break free of carbonite restraints, however, including <a href="/wiki/Galen_Marek" title="Galen Marek">Galen Marek</a> and <a href="/wiki/Starkiller_(clone)" title="Starkiller (clone)">his clone</a>. </p><p><a href="/wiki/Imperial_Knight" title="Imperial Knight">Imperial Knight</a>
