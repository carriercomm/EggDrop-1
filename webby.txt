you can even capture complete (scrolling) web pages from Internet Explorer.</span>  <span class="li">Easily annotate, highlight or obfuscate parts of the screenshot.</span>  <span class="li">Export the screenshot in various ways: save to file, send to printer, copy to clipboard, attach to e-mail, send Office programs or upload to photo sites like Flickr or Picasa, and others.</span><br /> <span class="li">&#8230;and a lot more options simplyfying creation of and work with screenshots every day.</li> </p> <p>Being easy to understand and configurable, Greenshot is an efficient tool for project managers, software developers, technical writers, testers and anyone else creating screenshots.</p> </div> <div class="two-col right-box"> <h2>Want to see more?</h2> <p>Of course there is more that Greenshot can do for you.
