</h3> <div class='post-header'> <div class='post-header-line-1'></div> </div> <div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-4281850618148149139' itemprop='description articleBody'> <span style="font-size: large;"><b>First of all, if you like Reddit website <span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: red;">STOP </span>right here.</span></b></span><br /> <span style="font-size: large;"><b><span style="background-color: white;">If you are their user or a fan leave your opinion for yourself until you read my side of the story.</span></b></span><br /> <span style="font-size: large;"><b><span style="background-color: white;"><br /></span></b></span> <span style="font-size: large;"><b><span style="background-color: white;">So my true story begins here:</span></b></span><br /> <span style="font-size: large;"><b><span style="background-color: white;"><br /></span></b></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>When I decided to make Cup Of Tips I also decided I needed to make my name availabe everywhere so you can find my posts at any time given.&nbsp;</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>I made Instagram account, Facebook page, Twitter... anything for my blog. But I also made a Reddit account so I can share tips &amp; tricks there too. I wasn't so active there because, honestly, I felt lost a bit there.&nbsp;</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>Just few weeks later one night my boyfriend came to me with this years Reddits' project: <b>Secret Santa</b>. He explained to me that they do this every year so you can feel holiday spirit from around the world. <b>All in all, for you who don't know this, it's a project where you were supposed to sign up, fill in basic informations about yourself (name, surname, adress, mobile phone number etc.) and wait until 1.12.2014. </b>when you will be 'matched' with someone random from around the world. If you get a person from ex. Canda you would have to send a gift to them. ANY GIFT. A plain postcard would do well. And you would be matched to some other random person from which you would get something.</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><b>At first, I was excited as never before and right away I singed up.</b></i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>I filled in everything I needed. Confirmed so many e-mails, look through my e-mails to see if there is anything new and so on.<b> I could never find enough words to describe my happiness and excitment about it</b>. I even brought in this my few close friends even tho one doesn't speak english at all. So, basicly I went through that all over again.</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><b>Now, next what am I going to write won't be because of greed or envy.</b></i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>For those who are reading me for the first time, I'm an artist and law student from a small country where people are getting fired a lot on daily basis. So due to that, I make money from my arts. It's cheap, unique and made with passion for art. And no it's not type of those painting or drawing with a pretty heart or a bunny.</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>Even tho, I don't have any money to buy someone a Mac laptop or band shirt, I would paint something for the person I would be matched to. <b>It would be free and made with love</b>. I was even planning to send a beautiful letter of inspiration which I wrote to him/her.</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>I just wanted to spread my art for a moment to the world. I wanted someone to have a real painting of mine which wasn't a picture and that is not here in my country.</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><b>1.12.2014. was finally here.</b></i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>But wait! where was my e-mail with my match? why nothing was showing on my Reddit account? why was there just 'error please contact support team'? <b>why did all my friends get someone and I didn't? why? what was going on?</b></i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><b><br /></b></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>There was an option where I could click on 'rematch' button. I clicked it but nothing! not even their e-mail!&nbsp;</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><b>I contacted their support team and kindly explained to them what was going on with my account and how I'm looking forward with my rematch.</b></i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>What did they say to me?&nbsp;</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>well, first of all, it was CLEARLY that human didn't read this. A machine was speaking to me. <b>All in all, they said there was an error with my account which they do not know and because of that can't fix it.&nbsp;</b></i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>Wait dear Mr. Machine how do you mean you can't fix this? I filled everything, I confirmed everything as all my friends and boyfriend but still nothing? Why does this website have so many users but such low support and control over their site?&nbsp;</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>I was sad. It brought tears and anger in me all at once. I calmed myself.</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>I wrote another letter to them and this one was on their official site.</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>Again, I kindly asked them what is the problem and can I help? Also, I asked them if this letter could be read by ANY HUMAN. I don't like speaking machines, they scare me. I want my fair match so I can give them my art and I want them this year.</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><b>Again... Nothing.</b></i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><b>Mr. Machine told me they can't do anything about it.</b></i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>Desperation. Sadness. Anger. Emotions hit me like a train that even my boyfriend was scared for me.</i></span></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i><br /></i></span></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="background-color: white;"><i>I may be a girl. I may be a drama queen. Call me anything you want.&nbsp;</i></span></span><br /> <i><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">But if I know one thing -<b>this wasn't fair to me</b></span>.</i><br /> <br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i><b>I wouldn't say a word today if I was one of the last members to join the project. Heck I singed up just 4 hours after official annoucment!</b></i></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i><b>I wouldn't say a word if my letters were read by a human.</b></i></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i><b>I wouln't say a word if I didn't confirmed anything.</b></i></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i><b>I woun't say a word if I gonna be with someone on Christmas day.</b></i></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i><br /></i></span> <i><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Yes, I have</span>&nbsp;<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">a lovley boyfriend, but he has his family too.</span></i><br /> <i><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Yes, I have my family also, but last 5 years I was alone because they go to the other town for Christamas.&nbsp;</span></i><br /> <i><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">No, I can't go with them, it's not that simple.</span></i><br /> <i><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span></i> <i><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><b>Secret Santa project was the only thing I was looking for this years Christmas.&nbsp;</b></span></i><br /> <i><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><b>Even tho, my Christmas day will be same as last five (basicly: eating microwave food, playing League Of Legends and watching Home Alone for the 100th time) I still had a hope that if I got just a plain paper from someone not from my country I would feel special.</b></span></i><br /> <i><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><b><br /></b></span></i> <i><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><b>Because someone tought of me. Someone cared for a second about me who is not my either boyfriend or a friend. Even tho they will never meet me. Heck even tho I didn't get anything I would still have a chance for my art.</b></span></i><br /> <i><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><b><br /></b></span></i> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;"><b><i>So with a tear in my eye,</i></b></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;"><b><i>I ask you dear Reddit why would you be so mean?</i></b></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;"><b><i>Why are you telling me that I can't be someones secret santa? Why are you ignoring me?</i></b></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;"><b><i><br /></i></b></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i>This probably isn't going to be read by anyone but atleast if one person not connected to them reads this it will mean something to me. To see how websites who present 'holiday lovley spirit' can be harsh and cruel. Even to a small unknow girl from even smaller uknown country.</i></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i><br /></i></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i>Nothing from now on will stop me. Not one website will ever again make me sad or lonley. We all deserve smile on our faces.</i></span><br /> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i><br /></i></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman,
