class="clear"></div></div> <div class="entry_body"><p>Nobody does pop culture parodies videos quite like YouTube sensations, and Nerdist Alliance members, <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/JckSparrow" target="_blank">The Hillywood Show®</a>, made up of comedy sister duo Hilly and Hannah Hindi. They&#8217;ve tackled everything from <em>Twilight</em> to <em>The Vampire Diaries</em> to <a href="http://www.nerdist.com/2014/05/doctor-who-parody-does-the-time-warp/" target="_blank"><em>Doctor Who</em> doing the Time Warp</a>. But there is one television behemoth, a monstrously popular genre program, that as yet the Hindi sisters hadn&#8217;t yet devoured&#8230; AMC&#8217;s staggering hit <a href="http://www.nerdist.com/tag/the-walking-dead/" target="_blank"><em>The Walking Dead</em></a>&#8230; UNTIL NOW THAT IS.</p> <p>We gave you <a href="http://www.nerdist.com/2014/10/preview-new-the-walking-dead-parody-from-the-hillywood-show/" target="_blank">the tiniest of previews</a> of this parody a few weeks ago, and now it&#8217;s finally here. Watch as Hilly gets grimy &#8211; Rick Grimes to be exact &#8211; and shoots the guts out of zombies to the tune of Queen&#8217;s biggest hit, &#8220;Another One Bites the Dust.&#8221; Quite a fitting soundtrack to so much undead slaughter. The production wanted to ensure authenticity, so The Hillywood Show® went on location for it.</p> <p>&#8220;Being fans of AMC&#8217;S <em>The Walking Dead</em>, we really wanted to create a parody that celebrates one of our favorite fandoms. We conceived the concept, took off with it, and ran all the way to Georgia to film! We shot the parody over a course of six days, and had an epic adventure. It was as amazing experience to let our obsession and love for the show run wild when creating <em>The Walking Dead Parody</em>.&#8221;</p> <p>You might also spot <em>Supernatural</em> actor and The Hillywood Show® fan Osric Chau making a cameo as a recognizable <em>The Walking Dead </em>character &#8211; keep your eyes peeled!</p> <p>Find out all the behind the scenes info about the six day shoot, which was written, directed, produced, edited (and more) by Hilly and Hannah in the video below!</p> <p><script type="text/javascript"
