But the classic 90s after-school snack has been given an all-American makeover.</p> <p>We&#8217;re big fans of pop-tarts in any flavor. What&#8217;s not to love about a toasted pastry?</p> <p>Some of the more zany flavours of Kellogg&#8217;s Pop-Tart have included Hot Fudge Sundae, Red Velvet, Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough, and even Birthday Cake.</p> <p>But combining a home-baked flaky sweet pastry pop-tart with salty peanut butter and savory bacon crumbs? Sorry Kellogg&#8217;s, that smashes you out of the park.</p> <p>Want a bite of the pop-tart action? Head to <a href="http://tedsbulletin14thstreet.com/" target="_blank">Ted’s Bulletin</a> in Washington DC for your hols.
