</div>              <div id="body">                 <p class="wptl top"><a href="http://go.theregister.com/tl/1232/-3467/remote-control-for-virtualized-desktops.pdf?td=wptl1232">Remote control for virtualized desktops</a></p>                   <p>Computer scientists at the University of New South Wales are teaching robots to rip apart computers for recycling.</p><p>Of course, while it's easy to use a machine to disassemble something, their trick is to do it while preserving key components. Which isn't quite as easy as it sounds.</p>     <div class="article_side_content not_moved">         <div id="article-mpu-container"><div id=ad-mu1-spot class=adu> <script> show_gpt('ad-mu1-spot'); </script> <noscript> <a href="http://pubads.g.doubleclick.net/gampad/jump?iu=/6978/reg_science/front&amp;sz=300x250%7C300x600&amp;tile=3&amp;c=33VG0EoqwQrMoAAAYiBCwAAAGd&amp;t=ct%3Dns%26unitnum%3D3%26unitname%3Dwww_top_mpu%26pos%3Dtop%26test%3D0" target="_blank"> <img src="http://pubads.g.doubleclick.net/gampad/ad?iu=/6978/reg_science/front&amp;sz=300x250%7C300x600&amp;tile=3&amp;c=33VG0EoqwQrMoAAAYiBCwAAAGd&amp;t=ct%3Dns%26unitnum%3D3%26unitname%3Dwww_top_mpu%26pos%3Dtop%26test%3D0" alt=""></a> </noscript> </div>           </div>     </div>   <p>The group, comprising researchers from UNSW's School of Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering and School of Computer Science and Engineering, started with LCD screens. The idea is to program self-learning routines into the robots, so that they can learn from their errors.</p><p>As project leader Professor Sami Kara says in the university's <a href="http://www.engineering.unsw.edu.au/news/robots-put-to-work-on-e-waste">release</a>, the robots “break one or two, but then they learn and they don't make the same mistake again”.</p><p>Not only that, but professor Kara also reckons once a robot's learned its way around a screen, it only needs a couple of minutes to disassemble it.</p><p>   <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fqLYVdVRvD8&amp;feature=youtu.be" data-media="x-videoplayer">Youtube Video</a> </p><p>“The idea is to remove the display and printed circuit board without damaging them because the rest can be recycled.”</p><p>The university hopes to attract participation in industry trials, which – given the amount of e-waste out there – Vulture South hopes isn't too hard to find.</p><p>The university says getting robots to handle e-waste has two aims: first, to deal with the build-up of products that laws say have to be recycled, but the recycling mechanisms can't keep up; and second, to reduce peoples' exposure to the often-hazardous chemicals in electronic products. ®</p>                 <p class="wptl btm"><a href="http://go.theregister.com/tl/1232/-3467/remote-control-for-virtualized-desktops.pdf?td=wptl1232">Remote control for virtualized desktops</a></p><script type="text/javascript">RegWpTl = ['<a href="http://go.theregister.com/tl/1252/-3489/internet-security-threat-report-2014.pdf?td=wptl1252">Internet Security Threat Report 2014<\/a>','<a href="http://go.theregister.com/tl/1213/-3422/secure-remote-control-for-conventional-and-virtual-desktops.pdf?td=wptl1213">Secure remote control for conventional and virtual desktops<\/a>','<a href="http://go.theregister.com/tl/1251/-3470/top-5-reasons-to-deploy-vmware-with-tegile.pdf?td=wptl1251">Top 5 reasons to deploy VMware with Tegile<\/a>','<a href="http://go.theregister.com/tl/1253/-3491/choosing-a-cloud-hosting-partner-with-confidence.pdf?td=wptl1253">Choosing a cloud hosting partner with confidence<\/a>','<a href="http://go.theregister.com/tl/1250/-3472/intelligent-flash-storage-arrays.pdf?td=wptl1250">Intelligent flash storage arrays<\/a>','<a href="http://go.theregister.com/tl/1254/-3490/beginners-guide-to-ssl-certificates.pdf?td=wptl1254">Beginner\'s guide to SSL certificates<\/a>','<a href="http://go.theregister.com/tl/1232/-3467/remote-control-for-virtualized-desktops.pdf?td=wptl1232">Remote control for virtualized desktops<\/a>'];</script>              </div>                                         <div id=article_body_btm>                  <div id="tips_or_corrections">                     <a href="mailto:corrections@theregister.co.uk?subject=%5Bcorrection%5D%20Boffins%20train%20robots%20to%20pull%20apart%20LCD%20screens%20%E2%80%93%20without%20breaking%20them&amp;body=re:%20http%3A%2F%2Fwww.theregister.co.uk/2014/11/18/boffins_train_robots_to_pull_apart_lcd_screens_without_breaking_them/"                         title="Spotted an error or have a tip?
