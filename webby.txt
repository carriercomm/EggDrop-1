The bodies of his parents, Nancy 64, and Michael 63, were found on Wednesday. Their deaths have been ruled as homicides.</p> <p>Petrozzino had recently moved in with his parents after splitting up with his wife, according to <a href="http://www.orlandosentinel.com/news/breaking-news/os-quad-amputee-sean-petrozzino-20141105-story.html">the Orland Sentinel</a>.</p> <p>A spokesman for the Orange Country Sheriff&#8217;s Office issued a statement saying &#8220;We do believe he has a firearm. He&#8217;s considered dangerous and they believe he is in the south Florida area.&#8221;</p> <p>Police think the amputee is hiding either in the Jupiter or Coral Springs are of Broward County.
