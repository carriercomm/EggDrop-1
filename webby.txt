Place in the refrigerator for at least 1 week and up to 3 weeks.</li></ol> <p><strong>To serve:</strong></p><ol> <li><span class="number_divider">1</span>The night before serving, place the frozen egg whites in the refrigerator to thaw.</li> <li><span class="number_divider">2</span>When ready to serve, let the egg whites come to room temperature. Gently whisk the eggnog together until just combined and no large pockets of whites or cream remain (do not overwhisk or you&#8217;ll deflate the eggnog).
