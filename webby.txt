World</h5>             <div class="post-author">Article by <a href='/author/admin/'>Urbanist</a>, filed under <a href='http://weburbanist.com/category/architecture/houses-residential/'>Houses &amp; Home in Poland is Now Thinnest in the World')">    </span>   </span></p> <p>Nestled in the interstitial space between two existing structures and raised up one story from the street sits an improbable structure, proposed then built as an artist-in-residence live/work dwelling of incredible audacity and engineered ingenuity.</p> <p>   <span class="pibfi_pinterest">   <img class="alignnone size-full Home in Poland is Now Thinnest in the World')">    </span>   </span></p> <p>   <span class="pibfi_pinterest">   <img title="skinny home room interiors" alt="" Home in Poland is Now Thinnest in the World')">    </span>   </span></p> <p>We have seen <a href="http://weburbanist.com/2007/10/05/cramped-urban-living-9-of-the-narrowest-city-houses-in-the-world/">skinny houses</a> and <a href="http://weburbanist.com/2010/06/06/narrower-towers-20-of-japans-thinnest-buildings/">other buildings</a> in places like Japan before, but no single-unit residence that matches this narrow wonder where even the bed is barely wide enough to sleep one.</p> <p>   <span class="pibfi_pinterest">   <img title="skinny home thin structure" alt="" src="http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/skinny-home-thin-structure.jpg" width="468" height="712" />    <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://weburbanist.com/2013/01/26/4-foot-wide-home-in-poland-is-now-thinnest-in-the-world/&media=http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/skinny-home-thin-structure.jpg&description=4-Foot-Wide Home in Poland is Now Thinnest in the World')">    </span>   </span></p> <p>Transparency, translucency and a tall angled roofs are essential to making the interior feel larger than it is. A bedroom, lounge, bathroom and office are all included at various levels, giving it a sense of separation despite all spaces running (by necessity) along the same plane.</p> <p>   <span class="pibfi_pinterest">   <img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-44210" title="skinny artist live work" alt="" src="http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/skinny-artist-live-work.jpg" width="468" height="800" />    <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://weburbanist.com/2013/01/26/4-foot-wide-home-in-poland-is-now-thinnest-in-the-world/&media=http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/skinny-artist-live-work.jpg&description=4-Foot-Wide Home in Poland is Now Thinnest in the World')">    </span>   </span></p> <p>Exposed cross-beams give each wall a sense of depth, and direct the eye up, down and out, enhancing the illusion of openness.
