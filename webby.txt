campus after allegedly taking drugs at a Halloween party.<br><br>Authorities say 18-year-old Stefan Sortland drove the ambulance from Fort Collins to nearby Loveland and crashed into at least one road sign. <br><br>The Coloradan reports the emergency vehicle is equipped with a GPS system and was tracked. <br><br>Police say they found Sortland standing next to the rig and wearing an EMT's vest. He now faces a number of charges including aggravated vehicle theft and drug possession.<br><br>Sortland allegedly stole the ambulance after taking cocaine and "molly," according to <a target="" rel="" href="http://www.thedenverchannel.com/news/front-range/fort-collins/ambulance-theft-suspect-stefan-sortland-says-he-took-drugs-at-halloween-concert">7NEWS in Denver</a>. <br><br>The 18-year-old was reportedly shot with a stun gun when police found him, and "stood on a bench, kicked the wall and masturbated" when he was taken to the police station, according to official reports.<br><br>Sortland is also accused of attacking two deputies while he was being booked into jail, punching one in the face and causing a concussion, while another deputy suffered a scrape and a
