id='show_per_page' />                 <div class="right" id='page_navigation'></div>                 <div id="paginated-content">                                  <div class="page">  <p>   If you are the messiest person you know, or think that your computer rig is straight out of the early 90s, you might want to take a look at this list. All of these gaming setups are incredibly impractical, but most also border on masochistic. Some of these people may look a lot like hoarders, some may look as though they don&#39;t own anything at all, including furniture, but from jury rigged desks to towers without cases, the majority of these photos will make you cringe and wonder, &quot;Where do people like this come from?&quot;</p>  <p>   For most people, gaming is a sort of religion and in that vein of thought, our desks are the temple. Aptly nicknamed, battlestations are where gamers play, eat, and even work for hours at a time. Unfortunately, not all of us can have the perfect setup, or even the most clean one, so take pity on the pictures you are about to view (most of which were taken from <a href="http://www.reddit.com/r/shittybattlestations">r/ShittyBattleStations</a>).</p>  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs1.JPG" style="width: 638px; height: 377px; " /></p>  <p>   &nbsp;</p>  <p>   Ah the classic CRT monitor, taking up 95% of the desk. While extending your desktop onto those two monitors, the resolution jumps to 1280x480. Also, I&#39;m pretty sure half of that computer is missing and the disc drive is trying to escape.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs2.JPG" /></p>  <p>   Chances are, if your laptop is being held up by empty tissue boxes, you used them all while crying about how poor you are.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs3.JPG" /></p>  <p>   Well, I can&#39;t see anything going wrong with this setup. It&#39;s the new &quot;open air&quot; model.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="Terrible" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs4.jpg" style="width: 638px; height: 559px; " /></p>  <p>   Painter&#39;s tape and a fan are all that&#39;s keeping this desktop from overheating. If only there was such a thing as an internal fan...</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs5.JPG" style="width: 638px; height: 479px; " /></p>  <p>   I don&#39;t want to know what magic was required to get this setup working, but what I do know is that it&#39;s evil.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs6.JPG" /></p>  <p>   This one is titled, &quot;Electrical Fire in the Making.&quot; Seriously, how do you even use that computer?</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs7.JPG" /></p>  <p>   When setting up a computer outside, you really have nothing to worry about. Nothing at all. The natural elements love electronics.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs8.JPG" /></p>  <p>   Fact: the most stable desks are built with loose pieces of wood balanced precariously on a stool.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs9.JPG" /></p>  <p>   Next time, maybe fit a case into your PC budget.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs10.jpg" style="width: 638px; height: 472px; " /></p>  <p>   It&#39;s a Nintendo rape dungeon, including a watchful Iwata portrait.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs11.JPG" /></p>  <p>   The tower of empty bottles moves closer by the day, inevitably consuming the whole computer within weeks.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs12.JPG" /></p>  <p>   The biggest feature of this cardboard desk is the complete lack of legroom.</p>  <p>   &nbsp;</p> </div> <div class="page">  <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs13.JPG" />  <p>   &nbsp;</p>  <p>   This gaming rig comes with a homeless person!</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs14.JPG" /></p>  <p>   Did your laptop screen mysteriously break off after a particularly awful round of League of Legends? This guy has you covered.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs15.jpg" /></p>  <p>   They might have a screen to protect against glare, but it&#39;s not doing much for the legroom. Also, my grandma wants her filing cabinet back.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs16.JPG" /></p>  <p>   I can think of at least ten ways that this setup can kill someone, including that cat.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs17.JPG" /></p>  <p>   Cinderblocks are like the Legos of desk building.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs18.JPG" /></p>  <p>   Well, all things considered, this guy has a fairly clean setup.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs19.JPG" /></p>  <p>   The dream of modern man is to be 5 inches thicker and have three speed settings. Unfortunately, that only applies to this laptop.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs20.JPG" /></p>  <p>   If you spend this much time in the bathroom, you should really see a doctor.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs21.JPG" /></p>  <p>   I didn&#39;t realize that case mods could also be wooden.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs22.JPG" /></p>  <p>   The dual CRT monitors are only one of the many offensive things in this photo.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs23.JPG" style="width: 638px; height: 359px; " /></p>  <p>   There&#39;s nothing like taking something originally intended to be portable, and turning it into a behemoth.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs24.JPG" /></p>  <p>   The collective weight of this setup is well over 200 pounds. That desk is a champ.</p> </div> <div class="page">  <p>   <img alt="" src="http://www.gameranx.com/img/12-Jun/bs25.JPG" /></p>  <p>   After inhaling this much ammonia and fecal matter, your game of Amnesia will be paired well with Toxoplasmosis.</p> </div> </div>                 </div>                                     <div class="left" style="margin:0px 0 10px 0;">     <!--<a
