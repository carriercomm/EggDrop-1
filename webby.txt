class="entry-content">       <p><em>By Matthew Manson, Writer/Director, WANNABE<br /> </em></p> <div id="attachment_1104" class="wp-caption alignleft" style="width: 278px"><a href="http://wannabefilm.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/xmastree.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-1104" title="Yes, Virginia, There Really Is a Chrismukkah Claus" src="http://wannabefilm.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/xmastree-268x300.jpg" alt="Yes, Virginia, There Really Is a Chrismukkah Claus" width="268" height="300" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Yes, Virginia, There Really Is a Chrismukkah Claus</p></div> <p>As a Jew during this, the holly-jolliest time of year, all thoughts lead to despair, self harm, and hours upon hours of ordering take-away Chinese food and watching &#8220;Seinfeld&#8221; reruns. You can NEVER see those enough times.</p> <p>But fret not, kind Jewish reader, for many of the warm songs echoing through the diaphragms of carolers have been written by &#8211; wait for it &#8211; Jews!</p> <p>&#8220;What? That&#8217;s CRAZY!&#8221; You exclaim. &#8220;Next you&#8217;ll be telling me the character of George Costanza was originally supposed to be Jewish!&#8221; (again, these are the types of references you make when you&#8217;ve been sitting around watching &#8220;Seinfeld&#8221; for too long).</p> <p>Lest you doubt me, allow me to list a &#8220;list&#8221; of Christmas-type songs either written or co-written by Jewish types.</p> <p><strong>The Christmas Song (Chestnuts Roasting on an Open Fire)</strong> &#8211; Writers Mel Torme and Bob Wells&#8230;Jewish!<br /> <strong>Rudolph the Red Nosed Reindeer, Holly Jolly Christmas and Rockin&#8217; Around the Christmas Tree</strong> &#8211; Johnny Marks was a Jew who specialized in Christmas songs.<br /> <strong>Do They Know It&#8217;s Christmas? (Feed the World)</strong> &#8211; Bob Geldof 1/4th Jewish&#8230;maybe a bit of a stretch, but he does <a href="http://www.urbanimage.tv/watermarked_images/fl_0491.jpg">look Jew&#8230;ish</a>.<br /> <strong>Santa Baby</strong> &#8211; written by Fred Ebb and Joan Javits (both Jews, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jacob_K._Javits">Javits of the famous family</a>)<br /> <strong>I&#8217;ll Be Home for Christmas</strong> &#8211; Walter Kent, who wrote the music and Kim Gannon, who co-wrote the lyrics&#8230;JEW!<br /> <strong>Silver Bells </strong>- Jay Livingston and Ran Evans&#8230;you guessed it&#8230;<br /> <strong>It&#8217;s the Most Wonderful Time of the Year</strong> &#8211; George Wylie (not his birth name) is also famous for writing the <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cfR7qxtgCgY">Gilligan&#8217;s Island Theme Song</a><br /> <strong>Sleigh Ride </strong>- Mitchell Parish who wrote the lyrics, was Jewish and born &#8220;Michael Hyman Pashelinsky&#8221; obviously.<br /> <strong>Let It Snow! Let It Snow! Let It Snow!</strong> &#8211; lyricist Sammy Cahn and music composer Jule Styne teh wuz Jewish.<br /> <strong>There&#8217;s No Place Like Home for the Holidays </strong>- Al Stillman, the lyricist&#8230;<br /> <strong>White Christmas</strong> &#8211; Irving Berlin wrote this one&#8230;or should I call him by his birth name Israel Isidore Baline? Nah, that just sounds silly.</p> <p>Here are a few neat articles that talk more in depth about this:</p> <p>New York Times &#8211; <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2009/12/18/opinion/18feinstein.html">&#8220;Whose Christmas Is It?&#8221;</a><br /> Los Angeles Times &#8211; &#8220;<a href="http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/news/la-et-jewish-musicians24-2009dec24,0,585406.story">Bob Dylan joins long list of Jewish musicians performing Christmas music&#8221;</a></p> <p>Oddly enough, Adam Sandler&#8217;s &#8220;Chanukah Song&#8221; was written by the Alabama chapter head of the Aryan Nation.</p> <h4 id="tweetandlike-heading">Wanna Share?</h4><div class="tweetandlike-container addthis_default_style"><div class="tweetandlike "><a href="http://twitter.com/share" data-title="Christmas+Songs+Written+By+Jews" data-url="http://wannabefilm.com/2011/12/christmas-songs-written-by-jews/" data-count="horizontal" data-text="Christmas Songs Written By Jews" data-lang="eng" data-via="wannabefilm" class="twitter-share-button">Tweet</a><script type="text/javascript" src="http://platform.twitter.com/widgets.js"></script></div><div class="tweetandlike"><g:plusone size= "medium"></g:plusone></div><div class="tweetandlike"><fb:like  href="http%3A%2F%2Fwannabefilm.com%2F2011%2F12%2Fchristmas-songs-written-by-jews%2F" send="" layout="standard" show_faces="true" action="like" font="tahoma" colorscheme="dark" width="150"></div></div> <!-- tweetandlike-container -->           </div><!-- .entry-content -->        <div class="entry-utility">       This entry was posted in <a href="http://wannabefilm.com/category/and-more/" title="View all posts in And More" rel="category tag">And More</a> and tagged <a href="http://wannabefilm.com/tag/christmas/" rel="tag">christmas</a>, <a href="http://wannabefilm.com/tag/christmas-songs-written-by-jews/" rel="tag">christmas songs written by jews</a>, <a href="http://wannabefilm.com/tag/hannuka/" rel="tag">hannuka</a>, <a href="http://wannabefilm.com/tag/hanukkah/" rel="tag">hanukkah</a>, <a href="http://wannabefilm.com/tag/jewish/" rel="tag">jewish</a>, <a href="http://wannabefilm.com/tag/jews/" rel="tag">jews</a>, <a href="http://wannabefilm.com/tag/songs/" rel="tag">songs</a>.
