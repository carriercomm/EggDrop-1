Unbeknownst to the family, movers had packed their kitty into a cardboard box, and she sailed out to Hawaii in a ship container without food and water.</p>             <p>"I couldn't find her anywhere," Ashley Barth tells <em>As It Happens</em> guest host Laura Lynch. "I figured maybe the moving company scared her and she took off or maybe she just didn't like it because there was no furniture in the home."</p>  <p>The family initially maintained hope that Mee Moowe would return to the house.</p>  <p>"We actually delayed our trip by three days," she says, "and we stayed in the house without furniture because my girls were just devastated."</p>  <div style="margin:20px 0;"><img style="width:100%;height:auto;display:block;" src="http://www.cbc.ca/asithappens/cat-survives-box-03.jpg "><div style="padding:10px;background:#ececec;color:#555555;">The cat was in Barth's family for nine years and was given as a birthday present to their then-two-year-old daughter. (Photo: Courtesy Ashley Barth)</div></div>  <p>Following their move, friends back in Suffolk checked the house for Mee Moowe every day.</p>  <p>Thirty-six days after packing up their home, the family's belongings arrived in Hawaii via ship, and movers began to unload their container.</p>  <p>"We heard the sound of a faint meow coming from a box... my heart immediately just sunk."</p>  <p>The cat meowed again.</p>  <p>"The girls got hysterical then, I got hysterical and everybody started screaming, 'Get her out, get her out!'"</p>  <p>One of Barth's daughter's pulled the emaciated and painfully thin Mee Moowe from the box. She had lost about half of her body weight.</p>  <p>"The movers actually taped her shut in a cardboard box... it makes me sick to think of how that happened, actually."</p>  <div style="margin:20px 0;"><img style="width:100%;height:auto;display:block;" src="http://www.cbc.ca/asithappens/cat-survives-box-01.jpg "><div style="padding:10px;background:#ececec;color:#555555;">Mee Moowe today. (Photo: Courtesy Ashley Barth)</div></div>  <p>Veterinarians believe Mee Moowe survived those 36 days without food and water due to being initially healthy and overweight before going into the box.</p>  <p>"She was extremely anemic and her phosphorous was very low. But after a lot of TLC and treatments from the vet, she will be okay."</p>  <p>Mee Moowe is currently in quarantine for three months because she missed her second round of required vaccinations before the move to Hawaii.</p> </div>        <!--                                 -->  <!-- START CURATED  OR RECENTS -->     <div id="curatedrecent" style="width: 100%"/>    <!-- END If tag curated-->   <!-- HIDE MOST RECENT IF THIS ENTRY-->                           <span style="color: #ffffff;">3905431</span>          <!-- END  HIDE MOST RECENT IF THIS ENTRY-->    <div id="mostrecents" class="sclt-recents">         <h2 style="font-size:16px">Most Recent</h2>    <!-- else if NOT  Episodes-->           <div class="mostrec hide386607"  category="NOT Episodes">        <a href="http://www.cbc.ca/asithappens/features/2014/12/03/woman-recalls-efforts-to-save-downed-soldier-at-war-memorial/"  title="Woman recalls efforts to save Cpl.
