item"><div class="contentinfo contentheader"><table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" width="100%"><tr class="wu_header"><td class="wu_type"><span class="type">(<a class="author">Sick Boy</a></strong>  </td> <td  style="text-align:right" class="wu_dtcreate"><small class="date" title="Sep 25 2003 at 3:59:20" >Thu Sep 25 2003 at 3:59:20</small></td> </tr></table>  </div> <div class="content"> <p>Of all the tricks and excuses I've heard, this is the only one that I use.  It has worked every time and it has worked for others.  Consider it a <a href="/title/Fiddler+on+the+Roof" title="Fiddler on the Roof" class='populated' >sacred tradition</a>, passed down through <a href="/title/word+of+mouth" title="word of mouth" class='populated' >word of mouth</a> from one person to the next, all in the name of <a href="/title/Damn+the+Man" title="Damn the Man" class='populated' >Damning the Man</a>.</p>  <p>This is <i><b>truly</b></i> how to get out of a ticket.</p>  <p>I don't often <a href="/title/How+to+speed+without+getting+a+speeding+ticket" title="How to speed without getting a speeding ticket" class='populated' >speed</a> but I do get pulled over a lot (although not recently.)  It's usually for running a <a href="/title/red+light" title="red light" class='populated' >red light</a> but it's has also been for <a href="/title/drunk+driving" title="drunk driving" class='populated' >driving drunk</a> (I wasn't actually drunk at the time, I'm just a bad driver.)  I tend to rock out too much to the music and watch the people more than the road.  This attracts the attention of <a href="/title/John+Q." title="John Q." class='populated' >John Q.</a> Lawman.</p>  <p><b>1 7</b>  t i m e s  p u l l e d   o v e r <b>0</b>  t i c k e t s  i s s u e d</p>  <p>Here is what you say:</p>  <big><big>"I'm sorry, officer, I have <a href="/title/diarrhea" title="diarrhea" class='populated' >diarrhea</a>."</big></big>  <p>Now, it's up to you to be convincing.  <a href="/title/the+Specials" title="the Specials" class='populated' >It's up to you</a>, to go the distance.  You might have to actually <a href="/title/Don%2527t+ever+lend+out+your+pants" title="Don't ever lend out your pants" class='populated' >shit your own pants</a> to avoid the ticket.  If you had my <a href="/title/history" title="history" class='populated' >track record</a> (seen above) you probably would (can you imagine if I had been ticketed every time.)</p>  <p><b>The Key:</b>  Be convincing.  This should include restlessness and pained looks.  Clutch your stomach but don't be <a href="/title/melodramatic" title="melodramatic" class='populated' >melodramatic</a>.  Offer for him to follow you and give you the ticket when you get home just, please God, let me get home so I don't ruin these seats.</p>  <p><b>Why it works:</b>  Two reasons.</p>  <p><b>1-</b> Everyone can relate to it.  Even cops, know the feeling of eating too many cherries and having to <a href="/title/speeding" title="speeding" class='populated' >squeal your tires</a> so you don't wreck shop in your trousers.  Whether it's <a href="/title/food+poisoning" title="food poisoning" class='populated' >food poisoning</a>, the flu or just <a href="/title/Jared" title="Jared" class='populated' >too much greasy food</a>, the officer's heart will soften.</p>  <p><b>2-</b> No one wants that on their <small>(<a href="/title/metaphorical" title="metaphorical" class='populated' >metaphorical</a>)</small> hands.  If you shit your pants and he didn't let you use the bathroom after you specifically told him your <a href="/title/getting+out+of+trouble" title="getting out of trouble" class='populated' >condition</a>, he can be in a lot of trouble.  Cops are constantly under <a href="/title/scrutiny" title="scrutiny" class='populated' >scrutiny</a> for their behavior and the one that just pulled you over does not want to have to answer to anyone as to why someone in his custody <a href="/title/bowel+disruptor" title="bowel disruptor" class='populated' >shat themselves</a>.  It's <a href="/title/Punching+customers+is+bad+for+business" title="Punching customers is bad for business" class='populated' >bad business</a>.</p>  </div> <div class="contentinfo contentfooter"><div
