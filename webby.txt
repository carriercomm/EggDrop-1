This year's winners include Barcelona, Copenhagen, and Helsinki.</p>  <p>Finally, a number of stories in 2014 examined the ways in which formerly unsustainable cities are remaking themselves. Walkable City</a></strong></p>  <p>The city that had long been the poster child of sprawl is putting its gears in reverse.
